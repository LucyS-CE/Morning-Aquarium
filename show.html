The Birth of King Penguin Babies

From October 1st to October 20th

About 13 king penguin babies were born in our aquarium this year.

It is exciting that babies are larger than their parents!

Only this time, you can see and feed the cute penguin babies.

We are waiting for you in King Penguin Home.
